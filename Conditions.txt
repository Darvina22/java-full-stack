ARITHMETIC OPERATORS
addition(+) to add two numbers
subraction(-) to subtract the given numbers
multiplication(*) to multiply the numbers
Division (/) Divides the left operand by the right operand
modulus(%) gives the remainder value
increment ++ 
pre increment,post increment
decrement--
post decrement , pre decrement



Assignment operators
= assign a value to variable
x+=y relevant to x=x=y
x-=y
x*=y
x/=y
x%=y




Relational operators
equal to(==)
not equal to(!=)
greater than(>)
lesser than(<)
grater than or equal to(>=)
lesser than or equal to(<=)


logical operators
and(&&) two are equal
or(||) one are equal
not(!) if condition is true it becomes false


bitwise operators
and(&) if both bits are 1 it becomes 1
or(|) if one bits has 1 it becomes 1
not(~) 1 turned into 0
